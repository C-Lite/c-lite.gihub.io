Our Services

What We Provide?

We onboard are a group of students came up with an idea to solve the problem of connectivity between students, teacher, and school management. Our Idea of CLITE brings up a revolutionary change in the functioning of school activity by digitalizing and virtualizing the classroom, Especially in this pandemic period of Covid-19, being ourselves as student-facing the same problem of classes and academics. With our App, we ensure that instead of wandering around between different Apps, students and teachers can easily communicate via online classes, chat groups, and discussion forums. We allow different interfaces and account types for students, teachers, and school management that provides various privileges and additional features for the various activity. CLITE provides a Dashboard for schedules, notices, and other various information and reminders. Teachers and School management can create Notices globally or for a particular audience with the feature of pinning the Notice. Clite provides an option to create chat rooms where teachers can communicate with the students, can share notes, assignments, and other resources via text messages. Along with it, CLITE provides a discussion forum where students can share doubts which can be answered by students and teachers. The entire App has designed entirely focusing on providing user-friendly interface and environment which can be used by anyone without having a prior tech knowledge.

Student Account

CLITE has an Especially designed interface for students providing them a comfortable and user-friendly environment for better understanding and efficient study. We have a well-described dashboard informing students about the schedule, notices, and reminders from and school and teachers. Easy to join chat rooms and discussion forums helping students to clear doubts efficiently and interactively.

Teacher Account

CLITE provides teachers special privileges for managing classes and various other activities. Teachers can create classrooms, chat groups, and also can generate Notices either Globally or for a particular audience. Teachers will be provided options to pin notices, send reminders, add students to chat rooms, or remove them with an ability to have an over-watch over the class. With such a user-friendly platform, Teachers can conveniently provide assignments, notes, and resources in the class chat rooms.

School Administration Account

CLITE provides school management a super account. With the user-friendly environment, this account has special privileges to have surveillance over every classroom, can check chat groups and discussion forums. The opening dashboard will display all the ongoing classes, notices, and reminders. Management can create global Notices and set reminders.
