Our Services

What We Provide?

clite enables affiliated teachers to set up an efficient and yet hassle-free online classroom with the best of features baked into a simple UI. What's more? clite connects private tutors with students for their tuitions with an innovative approach to tuition hunting. Students get the best of the app with the whole array of features meticulously designed for their use. Be it teachers from their coaching institutes, teachers from schools, or even their home tutors, a student gets one platform to excel with all. No more wandering for links - lectures, assignments or tests. clite keeps it all organised for you so you can focus on more what matters!



With clite we encourage you to take a huge step forward in revolutionising the tuition hunt process. The current system limits tutors from educating the vast majority of needy students out there. Likewise, it also limits students from learning from the best teachers that suit their learning pace, mode of study, and approachability. The tuition hunt process only involves reviews from a few neighbours or classmates. We bring clite Explore to the rescue! This section is built upon a wide database created using meticulous teacher profiling. This way students can look for tutors in thier locality based upon their preferences - teaching experience, fee, subjects offered, and more. Only let yourself decide your limit, join clite today!

Student Account

clite brings you an especially designed interface providing you a comfortable and user-friendly environment for better understanding and efficient study. We have a well curated dashboard informing you about your schedule, notices, and reminders from all of your teachers. Easy to join chat rooms and discussion forums help you clear doubts efficiently and interactively. We also let you participate first hand in the doubt solving process. Solve doubts of your classmates and get rewarded.

Affiliated Teacher Account

clite provides you special privileges for managing classes with ease. You can create classrooms, chat groups, share resources and generate notices for student across all of your courses. You will be provided with options to send reminders, add students to chat rooms, or remove them with an ability to monitor the class. With a user-friendly platform, designed for use by teachers of all ages, you can conveniently give assignments, share notes & study materials, conduct tests, resolve doubts and much more.

Private Teacher Account

For private tutors, we do much more. Apart from offering all of the above, clite connects you with students willing to join your tuitions with a revolutionised tuition hunt process. We connect, give you the tools to teach, and supervise, that's all! The fee is decided by you & your students, with no middlemen to complicate the process. We only charge for what we directly offer - our services, and that is a single-digit percentage cut per student fee.
